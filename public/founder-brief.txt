Product (Droplet)
-----------------
Category:    On-prem AI appliance for regulated SMBs
Thesis:      One on-device appliance runs the firm's storage, networking,
             and a local LLM under one auth boundary, with nothing leaving
             the building. Drafting, summarization, internal-document Q&A,
             automation — the same work staff would do in cloud SaaS, on
             a box that doesn't require an in-house IT team. Verticals:
             law, healthcare, real-estate firms with lending arms,
             architecture, small defense contractors. Prosumer/home tier
             follows in year 2.

Pillars:
 1. On-device by default. Nothing leaves the box unless the operator
    explicitly sends it.
 2. One auth boundary. One login, one update channel, one safety policy
    across the whole stack.
 3. Open-source by composition. No paid SaaS dependency in the critical
    path. The operator can leave at any time.
 4. AI safety by construction. An agent doctrine for hardware — irreversible
    operations are blocked for AI by design.
