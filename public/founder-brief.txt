Product (Droplet)
-----------------
Tagline:     On-prem AI for regulated SMBs without IT staff.

What it is:  One on-device appliance that plugs into the SMB network and
             replaces Dropbox, Microsoft 365 storage, Copilot, the router,
             and the Wi-Fi. Internal data never leaves the building.

How it works: Acts as the company brain. AI runs locally on an NPU and
              works as a unified agent across files, cameras, network,
              smart devices, and access control. Single chat interface
              tied to the team's existing identity.

Safety model: Three tiers.
              1. Reads run automatically.
              2. Writes (PoE toggles, SSID changes, camera management)
                 require user confirmation.
              3. Destructive operations (reboot, factory reset, severing
                 the management connection) are blocked by design.
